Libraries at the Centres

The mission of the Library is to provide information
services and access to full text both in digital and printed
resources to support the scholarly and information needs
of all students, faculty members and staff and to facilitate
creation of new knowledge.

The individual Centers of IPS have their own library.
Each library exclusively developed an excellent collection
of documents in area of Food Technology, Nutritional Sciences,
Media Studies, Computer Science and Fashion Design Technology.

The libraries are well equipped with modern facilities and
resources (Print and Electronic) in the forms of books, journals,
standards, theses, reports, etc. online databases and repositories
like INFLIBNET INDEST, DELNET, ETC. through which free online access
is provided to majority of the research journals within the premises.

IT has collection of about 28,500 items, used by more than 11,00 members.
Libraries provide access to over 40,000 online full- text journals and
several abstracting databases through university. Besides,
there are 500 e-books. The collection consists of Books, Back-volumes
of Periodicals, Theses, Conference Proceedings, Standards, Seminar and
Project Reports and their CDs.
